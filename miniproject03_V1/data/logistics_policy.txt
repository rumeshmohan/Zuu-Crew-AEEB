=== KAPRUKA LOGISTICS & POLICIES ===

COVERAGE AREA & PROMOTIONS:
- We deliver island-wide across all districts in Sri Lanka.
- Free Delivery: Available for ANY order totaling over Rs. 10,000, regardless of the district.

DELIVERY ZONES, FEES & TIMELINES:
1. COLOMBO CITY (Colombo 1 to Colombo 15):
   - Fee: Rs. 200
   - Time: Same Day or Next Day delivery.

2. GREATER COLOMBO & GAMPAHA DISTRICT:
   - Fee: Rs. 300
   - Time: 1 to 2 working days.

3. OUTSTATION - MAJOR CITIES (e.g., Kandy, Galle, Kurunegala, Matara):
   - Fee: Rs. 400
   - Time: 2 to 3 working days.

4. OUTSTATION - REMOTE DISTRICTS (e.g., Jaffna, Anuradhapura, Nuwara Eliya, Badulla, Trincomalee):
   - Fee: Rs. 500
   - Time: 3 to 5 working days.

IMPORTANT EXCEPTIONS & RULES:
- Fresh flowers and baked cakes can ONLY be delivered to Colombo, Gampaha, and Kandy districts.
  We do NOT deliver cakes or flowers to Remote Districts (e.g., Jaffna, Nuwara Eliya) as they will spoil.
- Standard items (gifts, toys, non-perishables) can be delivered island-wide.

RETURN & REFUND POLICY:
- Standard Items: Can be returned within 7 days of purchase.
- Perishables: Cakes, flowers, and fresh food items CANNOT be returned or refunded.
